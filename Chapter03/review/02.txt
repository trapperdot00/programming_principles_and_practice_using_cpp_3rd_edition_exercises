input is the data that the program receives
there can be inputs from multiple sources:
 - from the keyboard
 - from files
 - from other input devices
 - from other programs
 - from other parts of the program

an output is the calculated value from the inputs
there can also be outputs to multiple destinations:
 - to the screen
 - to files
 - to external devices
 - to other programs
 - to other parts of the program
